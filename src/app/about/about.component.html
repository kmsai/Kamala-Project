About us

Step Forward Serve The Humanity Reach Out and Help

Sri Late Chittaluri Kamala Devi(D.O.E 31/03/2021) was a simple and hardworking woman.She worked relentlessly to raise her three children when her husband Sri Chittaluri Venkateshwara Rao passed away in 1971.Though she received small amount for her savings as pension it was not enough to empower her children & adding to that, she was affected by polio at same time which made one of her legs a bit paralysed.Still her spirit was phenomoninal ,she fought with unfortunate situations with utmost will power and grit.Due to her good nature and kindheartedness She survived about 80+years and recently left us . We looked her evolution from a healthy adult to a lady in bedriddden condition.Our conscience questioned us "You had taken care of your loved one,but what about the ones who didnt receive the same love from their family mates" This ignited us to start an organisation which can assure them with moral and financial support.A small thought then ,now shaping into a big one with the support from near and dear ones.

Mission

It lies in taking care of two major age groups of our society aiming at the downtrodden elderly poeple and vulnerable children.Next comes the poverty stricken individuals & also disadvantaged animals. Our vision with the organisation is to support these impoverished and abandoned orphans ,old age people ,underprivileged children hailing especially from lower income backgrounds.On the other spectrum,animals which go through tough situations without food and medical help are to be taken care of. Our vision to transform the quality of life of these individals through greater understanding, collaboration and by endorsing a lasting, mutually life-enhancing relationships.

Vision

The world from the shocks of COVID19 is slowly picking up its pace but not at a required speed.Our mission complements with the current situation.we pledge to support & empower & provide medical backing ,groceries ,food,educational supplies ,clothes etc to the penniless individuals,less fortunate & needy ones who have none to look after & are in hope of some aid. In these times, the individuals as well as animals have no sources of food nor are well-wishers and caregivers allowed to step out to feed them. So,our mission is to compassionately serve them. Nevertheless we facilitate, organize, and promote cooperation from the volunteers willing to extent their help for the cause.
